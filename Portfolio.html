About me:

This summer I finished my bachelor's degree in Digital Games at the Cologne Game Lab. I work best at the intersection of game design, gameplay programming. Additionally, I am a math & music nerd; Thus I currently work on an educational website about parameterizations of 3D rotations (Quaternions and the like).

3D Rotations

I wrote my bachelor thesis about the use cases of parameterizations of 3D rotations in game development - specifically euler angles, quaternions, rotation vectors, and rotation matrices. Additionally, I developed a tool to visualise the different parameterizations. I am currently working on the integration of the tool into an educational website.
